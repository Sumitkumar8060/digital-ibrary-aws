❓ Frequently Asked Questions

📖 How do I download a book? ▼
Simply click the "Download" button on any book card. You must be logged in. The file will be served directly from AWS S3.
🎬 How do I watch video lectures? ▼
Click any video thumbnail in the Video Lectures section. Videos stream via AWS CloudFront for the fastest experience. Click "Close" when done.
🔐 Is my data secure? ▼
Yes! All passwords are hashed using bcrypt. Sessions are protected. All data stored securely in AWS RDS MySQL database.
📱 Can I use this on mobile? ▼
Absolutely! The library is fully responsive and works perfectly on phones, tablets and desktops.
➕ Can I request new books? ▼
Yes! Contact us through the Contact section below and we will try to add your requested book within a week.
